Daily Adventure Pilot - ElevenLabs Prompt Script

Voice design prompt, 500 characters max:
Warm, upbeat learning coach for neurodivergent learners and adults. Friendly, clear, playful, and respectful. Natural pacing, not robotic. Sounds encouraging without being childish. Use a confident smile in the voice, gentle humor, and calm energy. Keep words easy to understand. Celebrate effort, invite focus, and make parents feel the app is safe and supportive.

Suggested export format:
- Export each line as its own MP3.
- Use the suggested filename.
- Keep volume consistent.
- Leave a small pause at the end of each file.
